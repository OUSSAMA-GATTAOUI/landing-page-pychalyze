PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Pichalyze ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use our desktop application and website.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a Pichalyze account we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your actual password)
  • Account creation date and last login date
  • Your subscription plan (Free or Pro)

We do NOT collect any of the data files you open or analyse in the app. Your CSV, Excel, JSON and Parquet files never leave your computer.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Sending you email verification codes
  • Managing your subscription status
  • Responding to support requests you send us

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Your account information is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon (neon.tech). The database is located in the EU (Frankfurt). Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256 with 260,000 iterations — they cannot be reversed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any third party, except:

  • Paddle — our payment processor and Merchant of Record. When you purchase a Pro plan, your payment is processed by Paddle. We may receive your email address and subscription status to activate your Pro access. Paddle's privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Update your account information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at gattaouioussama@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

7. Security

We take security seriously. Your password is never stored in plain text. All database connections use SSL encryption. The app configuration is XOR-encrypted so your credentials cannot be read from the distributed files.

8. Children

Pichalyze is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
gattaouioussama@gmail.com

This policy applies to the Pichalyze desktop application and the Pichalyze landing website.
